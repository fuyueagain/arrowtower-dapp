Project Introduction

ArrowTower is a location check-in platform based on the Polkadot ecosystem, supporting one-click platform deployment and multiple usage scenarios. Through zero Gas fee backend proxy minting technology, users can experience Web3 without barriers. After tourists complete featured route check-ins and interactive tasks, the system automatically issues unique NFT digital souvenirs without requiring users to understand Gas fees or make payments, learning wallet usage and signing.
